Why CoSMIC?

Shifting from reactive agents to human-like reasoning intelligence.

Challenge: Purely Reactive

Traditional models lack explicit mechanisms to reason over past experiences when facing unfamiliar contexts.

Solution: Dual-Process Memory

CoSMIC integrates Working, Short-term, and Long-term Memory to emulate brain-like reasoning processes.

Challenge: Opaque Black-Box

Most existing frameworks lack transparency, making it hard to interpret why a specific trajectory was chosen.

Solution: Interpretability

Our system provides traceable decision-making by linking perceptual features to behavior semantic axes.

Architecture Overview

Integrating tripartite memory system with dual-process reasoning.

CoSMIC integrates a tripartite memory system (STM, LTM, WM) with dual-process reasoning. Heuristic and analytic processes work together to ensure responsive and deliberative decision-making.

Award Ceremony at ICCV 2025

Honolulu, Hawaii - Oct 19, 2025

In September 2025, our team participated in the End-to-End Driving Challenge held in conjunction with the ICCV Workshop. Organized by prestigious institutions including OpenDriveLab and NVIDIA Research, this international competition attracted top-tier participants from both industry and academia, including leaders such as NVIDIA, BOSCH, and Xiaomi, alongside elite research teams from the U.S., Korea, China, Sweden, and beyond. Centered on the NAVSIM v2 dataset, the challenge evaluated the decision-making capabilities of models in realistic driving scenarios. Performance was measured using the EPDMS metric, providing a comprehensive assessment of safety, comfort, regulatory compliance, and generalization ability.
